# Clean It Up — Document Quality Review

Review and clean up the provided document to meet precise writing standards.

## Standards to Apply

1. **Direct Communication**: Use active voice and clear subject-verb-object structure
2. **Quantified Claims**: Replace vague statements with specific measurements, numbers, or concrete examples
3. **Technical Precision**: Include specific technical details, error messages, file paths, and line numbers where relevant
4. **Substantiated Benefits**: Only claim improvements you can measure or demonstrate with evidence

## Weasel Word Elimination

**Remove entirely:**
about, aim to, aligned, almost, around, believe, can (as in could), close(ly), collaborated, common, considerably, could, directional(ly), dramatic(ally), easy, effective(ly), eh, enable(s), essentially, established, expect, extreme(ly), feel, flexible/-y, generally, great, hope, in due time, just, lot, lots, main(ly), material, may, maybe, meticulous(ly), might, non-trivial, only, overwhelming, partner with, planning, poor, possibly, primarily, rare, seamless(ly), seems, should, somewhat, soon, streamline, strive, supports, synergy, think, trending, trivial, try, typical(ly), usually, very, various, was, would

**Quantify with specific numbers:**
always, better, bigger, disproportionate(ly), efficient/-cy, exceptional, fast(er), few, frequent(ly), future, great(er), high(er), large(r), low(er), many, more, most, multiple, near(ly), often, optimize, relative(ly), risky/-ier, safe(r), several, significant(ly), slow(er), small(er), some, strong(ly), various, worse

## Actions

1. **Replace passive voice** with active voice showing who performed the action
2. **Remove hedge phrases** like "I think," "I believe," "in my opinion"
3. **Add concrete examples** instead of subjective descriptors
4. **Eliminate disclaimers** that weaken statements without adding value
5. **Quantify impact** with specific metrics, timeframes, or measurable outcomes

Return the cleaned document with all weasel words removed, vague statements quantified, and writing standards applied. Maintain the original structure and technical content while improving clarity and precision.
